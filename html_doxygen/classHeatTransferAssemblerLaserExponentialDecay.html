Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class HeatTransferAssemblerLaserExponentialDecay< dim >

Class that assembles the laser heating as a volumetric source for the heat transfer solver. Exponentially decaying model is used to simulate the laser heat source: "Liu, S., Zhu, H., Peng, G., Yin, J. and Zeng, X.,.

  1. Microstructure prediction of selective laser melting AlSi10Mg using finite element analysis. Materials & Design, 142, pp.319-328."
