30 /**
31  * Implementation of a gear3 scheme for the integration
32  * of the particle motion. Note that reinitilization of force and torque is also
33  * integrated into integration class
34  *
35  * @note Gear3 is a third-order predictor-correcton integration scheme. In gear3,
36  * acceleration, velocity and position of particles are updated using the
37  * following method. b is the first derivative of acceleration
38  *
39  * Prediction:
40  * b(n+1,prediction) = b(n)
41  * a(n+1,prediction) = a(n) + b(n) * dt
42  * v(n+1,prediction) = v(n) + a(n) * dt + 1/2 * b(n) * dt ^ 2
43  * x(n+1,prediction) = x(n) + v(n) * dt + 1/2 * a(n) * dt ^ 2 + 1/6 * b(n) * dt
44  * ^ 3
45  *
46  * a(n+1,correction) = F(n+1) / m
47  * delata_a(n+1) = a(n+1,correction) - a(n+1,prediction)
48  *
49  * Correction:
50  * ┌ ┐ ┌ ┐ ┌ ┐
51  * | x(n+1,correction) | | x(n+1,prediction) | | eta1 * dt ^ 2 |
52  * | v(n+1,correction) | = | v(n+1,prediction) | + | eta2 * dt | * delata_a(n+1)
53  * | a(n+1,correction) | | a(n+1,prediction) | | eta3 |
54  * | b(n+1,correction) | | b(n+1,prediction) | | eta4 * dt ^ -1 |
55  * └ ┘ └ ┘ └ ┘
56  * where eta1 - eta4 are gear3 method coefficients, eta1 = 1/12, eta2 = 5/12,
57  * eta3 = 1, eta4 = 1
58  *
59  * @author Shahab Golshan, Bruno Blais, Polytechnique Montreal 2019-
60  */
