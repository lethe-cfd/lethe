template<int dim, typename VectorType >
std::pair<double, double> calculate_fluid_and_particle_volumes ( const DoFHandler< dim > &  void_fraction_dof_handler,
const VectorType &  present_void_fraction_solution,
const Quadrature< dim > &  quadrature_formula,
const Mapping< dim > &  mapping 
)

This function calculates total volume of fluid and total volume of particles in cfd-dem simulation.

\(V_{fluid} = \int \varepsilon d \Omega \), \(V_{particles} = \int (1- \varepsilon) d \Omega \) where \( \varepsilon \) is the void fraction and \( d \Omega \) is the volume of the cell.

Parameters
void_fraction_dof_handler.Used to calculate the void fraction at quadrature points
present_void_fraction_solution.Void fraction solution vector
quadrature_formula.Quadrature formula.
mappingThe mapping of the simulation
