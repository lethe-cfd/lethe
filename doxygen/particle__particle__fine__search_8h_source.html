38 /**
39  * This class is used for local-local and local-ghost fine particle-particle
40  * contact search. Fine search is used to find all the particle pairs which are
41  * physically in contact and obtain all the required information for calculation
42  * of the contact force
43  *
44  * @note
45  *
46  * @author Shahab Golshan, Polytechnique Montreal 2019-
47  */

52 public:
53  ParticleParticleFineSearch();
54 
55  /**
56  * Iterates over a vector of maps (pairs_in_contact) to see if the particles
57  * which were in contact in the last time step, are still in contact or not.
58  * If they are still in contact it will update the collision info, including
59  * tangential overlap, based on new properties of the particle pair, if they
60  * are not in contact anymore it will delete the pair from the
61  * pairs_in_contact and also its information from pairs_in_contact_info.
62  * Then it iterates over the contact candidates from broad search to see if
63  * they already exist in the pairs_in_contact or not, if they are not in the
64  * pairs_in_contact and they have overlap, the pair will be added to the
65  * pairs_in_contact and its contact information will be stored in the
66  * corresponding element of the pairs_in_contact_info
67  *
68  * @param particle_container A container that is used to obtain iterators to
69  * particles using their ids
70  * @param adjacent_particles A map of maps which stores all the required
71  * information for calculation of the contact force of particle pairs
72  * @param contact_pair_candidates The output of broad search which shows
73  * contact pair candidates
74  * @param neighborhood_threshold A value which defines the neighbor particles
75  * @param periodic_offset A tensor of the periodic offset to change the
76  * particle location of the particles on the periodic boundary 1 side,
77  * the tensor as 0.0 values by default
78  */
79 
80  void
81  particle_particle_fine_search(

83  &particle_container,

85  &adjacent_particles,
