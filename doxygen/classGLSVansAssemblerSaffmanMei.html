Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class GLSVansAssemblerSaffmanMei< dim >

Class that assembles the Lift force using Saffman-Mei model.

This implementation follows the formulation in the book "Multiphase Flows with Droplets and Particles" by Crowe et al. (2011) and the brief communication article "An approximate expression for the shear lift force on a spherical particle at finite reynolds number" by Mei (1992)
