28 /**
29  * Manages removing repetitions and adding new contact pairs to the contact
30  * containers when particles are exchanged between processors. If the contact
31  * pair (in adjacent particles containers) does not exist in the output of the
32  * new (current step) broad search, it is removed from the contact pair
33  * (adjacent containers), since it means that the contact is being handled by
34  * another processor. If the pair exists in the output of the new broad search,
35  * it is removed from the output of the broad search, as the contact is already
36  * being processed. This process is performed for many types of particle-object
37  * pairs : local-local particle-particle pairs, local-ghost particle-particle
38  * pairs and particle-wall pairs, particle-floating wall pairs and
39  * particle-floating mesh contacts pairs. Note : contact_type is important for
40  * local or ghost particle-particle contact only
41  *
42  * @param pairs_in_contact adjacent particle-object pairs from fine search at last time step
43  * @param contact_candidates particle-object contact pairs from board search at current time step
44  * @param contact_type label of contact type to apply proper manipulation of contact removal in containers
45  */
46
