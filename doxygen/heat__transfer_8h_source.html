63 /**
64  * @brief Implementation of heat transfer as an auxiliary physics. The heat
65  * equation is weakly coupled to the velocity field. Equation solved:
66  * \f$ \rho C_p \cdot \left( \frac{dT}{dt} + u \cdot \nabla T \right) =
67  * k \cdot \nabla^2 T + \tau : \nabla u \f$
68  *
69  */
