16  *
17  * Implementation of heat transfer as an auxiliary physics.
18  * This heat equation is weakly coupled to the velocity field.
19  * Equation solved:
20  * rho * Cp * (dT/dt + u.gradT) = k div(gradT) + nu/rho * (gradu : gradu)
21  *
22  * Polytechnique Montreal, 2020-
23  */

74  {
