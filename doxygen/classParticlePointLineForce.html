Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class ParticlePointLineForce< dim >

Calculation of the non-linear particle-point and particle-line contact force using the information obtained from the fine search and physical properties of particles and walls. Since the inputs and the calculation method of particle-point and particle-line contact forces are similar, we only used one function for both tasks
