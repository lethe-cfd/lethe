Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class GLSNavierStokesCahnHilliardAssemblerCore< dim >

Class that assembles the core of the Navier-Stokes equation with free surface using Cahn-Hilliard modeling. According to the following weak form: \( \nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} + \rho \mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla \mathbf{u} + \nabla p - \mu \nabla\cdot (\nabla \mathbf{u} + (\nabla \mathbf{u})^T)=0 \) with an SUPG and PSPG stabilization.
