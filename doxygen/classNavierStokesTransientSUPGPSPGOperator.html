template<int dim, typename number>

Perform cell integral on a cell batch without gathering and scattering the values, and according to the Jacobian of the discretized transient Navier-Stokes equations with SUPG/PSPG stabilization.

Parameters
[in]integratorFEEvaluation object that allows to evaluate functions at quadrature points and perform cell integrations.

The expressions calculated in this cell integral are: (q,∇δu) + (v,∂t δu) + (v,(u·∇)δu) + (v,(δu·∇)u) - (∇·v,δp) + ν(∇v,∇δu) (Weak form Jacobian) + (∂t δu +(u·∇)δu + (δu·∇)u + ∇δp - ν∆δu)τ·∇q (PSPG Jacobian) + (∂t δu +(u·∇)δu + (δu·∇)u + ∇δp - ν∆δu)τu·∇v (SUPG Jacobian Part 1) + (∂t u +(u·∇)u + ∇p - ν∆u - f )τδu·∇v (SUPG Jacobian Part 2).
